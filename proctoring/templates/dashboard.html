Exam Guidelines

  • Device and Internet: Ensure your device is fully charged and connected to a high-speed internet connection.
  • Webcam and Microphone: Mandatory for real-time monitoring; must remain on throughout the session.
  • No Unauthorized Tabs: Opening other browser tabs or applications will trigger a security flag.
  • Stay in Frame: Your face must be clearly visible to the AI proctor at all times.
  • Quiet Environment: Background noise or movement by other people may lead to disqualification.
  • Individual Effort: Any form of collaboration is strictly prohibited.
  • Behavior Monitoring: AI systems will flag suspicious activities for human proctor review.
  • Consequences: Violations may lead to immediate termination of your exam.
